General Information

MOD Title: Custom Policy
Version: v1.2 for Snitz Forums v3.4.06
Author: Michael Reisinger (OneWayMule)
Last Update: September 30, 2006

Description
 Enables you to easily create your own policy which is displayed when a user registers at your forum.

Included Files:
 admin_policy.asp  (Custom Policy administration area)
 dbs_policy.asp  (Contains the information for a fresh install with MOD Setup)
 dbs_policy_uninstall.asp  (Contains the information for an uninstallation with MOD Setup)
 inc_policy.asp  (the new policy file for Snitz v3.4.06)
 policy.asp  (the new policy file for Snitz v3.4.xx)
 readme.html  (This file)

Upgrade Instructions

  1. Upgrade from v1.1
    Upload all files (admin_policy.asp, inc_policy.asp, policy.asp) from the zip to your forum folder
    That's it!
    Do NOT run MOD Setup!


  2. Upgrade from v1.0
    Upload all files (admin_policy.asp, inc_policy.asp, policy.asp) from the zip to your forum folder
    That's it!
    Do NOT run MOD Setup!

top

Uninstall

  1. Undo the changes you've done to admin_home.asp

  2. Snitz v3.4.06:
    Upload admin_home.asp and a unmodified inc_policy (from the Snitz zip file) to your forum folder

    Previous Snitz versions (v3.4 - v3.4.05):
    Upload admin_home.asp and a unmodified policy.asp (from the Snitz zip file) to your forum folder

  3. Remove admin_policy.asp from your forum folder

  4. Logon as admin and click on "Admin Options"
    Click on "MOD Setup"
    Choose "Uninstall - MOD: Custom Policy v1.2" from the dropdown box and hit "Update"
